🎨 VISUAL MODES:

--visual-mode waterfall   (default, colored blocks)
--visual-mode matrix      (Matrix-style falling data)

--visual-mode binary      (0s and 1s)
--visual-mode spectrum    (byte distribution)
--visual-mode waveform    (ASCII waveform)

🌈 COLOR MAPS:

--colormap rainbow        (default, full spectrum)
--colormap heat           (heat map colors)
--colormap grayscale      (black and white)
--colormap binary         (pure black/white)
